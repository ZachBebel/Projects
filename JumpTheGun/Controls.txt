Controls:
	
Movement:
W - Move Forward
A - Move Left
S - Move Backward
D - Move Right
SPACE - Jump
	
Camera:
Mouse - Look and Rotate
	
Actions:
Mouse Left-Click - Fire Bullet
Mouse Right-Click - Teleport to most recently fired Bullet, Click and hold to follow Bullet

GUI:
P - Pause
L - Lock Cursor
ESC - Unlock Cursor
